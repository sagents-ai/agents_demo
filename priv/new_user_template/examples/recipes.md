## Pasta Carbonara
**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 20 minutes

### Ingredients
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g pancetta or guanciale
- 4 large eggs
- 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
- Black pepper, freshly ground
- Salt for pasta water

### Instructions
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente
2. Meanwhile, fry pancetta until crispy
3. Beat eggs with grated cheese and pepper
4. Drain pasta (reserve 1 cup pasta water)
5. Toss hot pasta with pancetta, then remove from heat
6. Quickly stir in egg mixture (heat from pasta cooks eggs)
7. Add pasta water if needed for creaminess

## Simple Tomato Sauce
**Serves:** 4-6 | **Time:** 30 minutes

### Ingredients
- 800g canned whole tomatoes
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- Fresh basil leaves
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Pinch of sugar (optional)

### Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute)
3. Add tomatoes (crush by hand as you add)
4. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally
5. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar if needed
6. Tear basil leaves and stir in before serving
